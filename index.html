About

My coding journey started when I was 14 with Pascal.
Me and a couple of students were put in charge to design a new website for my High School. It took me a few lines of code to understand that this is the future of technology and I loved doing it. Back then it was very hard in Albania to find a career in IT since it had barely started. I decided to go to Med School and after 5 years I moved to the States.
After spending more than a decade working in hospitality, medical and dental setting for multimillion dollar brands and startups, I still decided to go back to doing what I always loved, coding.
Here I am in this exciting journey creating my first website, building my first projects and getting myself ready to know more and more people in this amazing field.
